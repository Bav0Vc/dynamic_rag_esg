# Questions

## \#1

The undertaking shall disclose its estimated gross greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions in tons of CO2 equivalent (tCO2eq) considering the content of the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard (version 2004), including: the Scope 1 GHG emissions in tCO2eq (from owned or controlled sources).

---

What are the company's total Scope 1 GHG emissions in tonnes of CO₂ equivalent for the most recent reporting year?

## \#2

The undertaking shall disclose its estimated gross greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions in tons of CO2 equivalent (tCO2eq) considering the content of the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard (version 2004), including: the location-based Scope 2 emissions in tCO2eq (i.e. emissions from the generation of
purchased energy, such as electricity, heat, steam or cooling).

---

What are the company's total Scope 2 (location-based) GHG emissions in tonnes of CO₂ equivalent?

## \#3

The undertaking shall disclose its total energy consumption in MWh, with a breakdown as per the table below, if it can obtain the necessary information to provide such a breakdown:

- Electricity (as reflected in utility billings) -> Renewable, Non-renewable, total
- Fuels -> Renewable, Non-renewable, total
- Total -> Renewable, Non-renewable, total

---

What is the company's total energy consumption in MWh, and what share comes from renewable sources?

## \#4

The undertaking shall disclose its GHG intensity calculated by dividing ‘gross greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions’ disclosed under paragraph 30 by ‘turnover (in Euro)’ disclosed under paragraph 24(e)(iv)5.

---

What is the GHG intensity of the company, expressed as tonnes of CO₂ equivalent per euro of turnover?

## \#5

The undertaking shall disclose the number and area (in hectares) of sites that it owns, has leased, or manages in or near a biodiversity sensitive area.

---

How many sites does the company own, lease or manage that are located in or near a biodiversity-sensitive area, and what is their total surface area in hectares?

## \#6

The undertaking shall disclose its total water withdrawal, i.e. the amount of water drawn into the boundaries of the organisation (or facility); in addition, the undertaking shall separately present the amount of water withdrawn at sites located in areas of high water-stress.

---

What is the company's total annual water withdrawal in cubic metres, and how much of this comes from sites in areas of high water stress?

## \#7

The undertaking shall disclose: the total annual generation of waste broken down by type (non-hazardous and hazardous);

---

What is the total annual waste generated by the company, broken down into hazardous and non-hazardous waste?

## \#8

The undertaking shall disclose: he total annual waste diverted to recycling or reuse;

---

What percentage or volume of the company's total waste is diverted to recycling or reuse?

## \#9

The undertaking shall disclose the number of employees in headcount or full-time equivalent for the following metrics: type of employment contract (temporary or permanent);

---

How many employees does the company have in total, and what is the breakdown by type of employment contract (permanent vs. temporary)?

## \#10

The undertaking shall disclose the number of employees in headcount or full-time equivalent for the following metrics: gender;

---

What is the gender breakdown of the company's workforce?

## \#11

The undertaking shall disclose the following information regarding its employees: the number and rate of recordable work-related accidents;

---

What is the rate of recordable work-related accidents per 200,000 hours worked?

## \#12

The undertaking shall disclose the following information regarding its employees: the number of fatalities as a result of work-related injuries and work-related ill health.

---

How many fatalities resulted from work-related injuries or work-related ill health during the reporting period?

## \#13

The undertaking shall disclose: whether the employees receive pay that is equal or above applicable minimum wage for the
country it reports in, determined directly by the national minimum wage law or through a
collective bargaining agreement;

---

Do all employees receive pay equal to or above the applicable national minimum wage?

## \#14

The undertaking shall disclose: the percentage gap in pay between its female and male employees. The undertaking may
omit this disclosure when its headcount is below 150 employees noting that this threshold
will be reduced to 100 employees from 7 June 2031;

---

What is the percentage gender pay gap between female and male employees?

## \#15

The undertaking shall disclose: the percentage of employees covered by collective bargaining agreements;

---

What percentage of employees are covered by collective bargaining agreements?

## \#16

The undertaking shall disclose: the average number of annual training hours per employee, broken down by gender.

---

What is the average number of annual training hours per employee, broken down by gender?

## \#17

In case of convictions and fines in the reporting period, the undertaking shall disclose the number
of convictions, and the total amount of fines incurred for the violation of anti-corruption and anti-
bribery laws.

---

How many convictions and what total amount of fines did the company incur for violations of anti-corruption and anti-bribery laws during the reporting period?

## \#18

If the undertaking has established GHG emission reduction targets, it shall disclose its targets in absolute values for Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions. In line with paragraphs 50 to 53 above and if it has set Scope 3 reduction targets, the undertaking shall also provide targets for significant Scope 3 emissions. In particular, it shall provide: the target year and target year value; the base year and base year value;

---

What GHG emission reduction targets has the company set for Scope 1 and Scope 2, including the target year and base year values?

## \#19

The undertaking shall disclose an answer to the following questions.

a) Does the undertaking have a code of conduct or human rights policy for its own workforce? (YES/NO)
b) If yes, does this cover:
i. child labour (YES/ NO);
ii. forced labour (YES/ NO);
iv. discrimination (YES/NO);

---

Does the company have a code of conduct or human rights policy for its workforce, and does it cover child labour, forced labour, and discrimination?

## \#20

If the undertaking has a governance body in place, the undertaking shall disclose the related gender diversity ratio.

---

What is the gender diversity ratio in the company's governance body (ratio female to male members)?
